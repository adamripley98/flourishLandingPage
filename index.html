Who we are

Flourish aims to empower sustainable improvements in local communities through a micro-donation and donor analytics platform, which enables our users to create positive change for causes about which they feel passionately.

Flourish is designed to link millennial donors to the non-profit organizations that they care about. Research has shown that we are more willing to give, but prefer to give smaller amounts more frequently than our parents' generation. Given the operational difficulties of handling frequent small donations, we want to provide a full product - from a donor mobile app to web-based analytics - that enables nonprofits to successfully attract, understand, and cultivate relationships with a new generation of givers.

Who we are

There are three problems that we seek to address: (1) Millennials are unable to and do not donate large amounts of money; (2) NPOs cannot manage many small transactions from donors; (3) NPOs demand for an increasing amount of steady inflows of cash for operations and growth.

Over our last two years in college, we have noticed two growing movements of micro investing and social impact consumption. Micro investing allows people with as little as $1 to invest and has targeted Generation Y. Services like Acorns and Robinhood have been able to reduce the barriers to investing; we hope to do the same for non-profit donations. It has been proven that millenials, generation Y, and Z are much more willing to give than older generations, but give very differently than older donor bases. They tend to give smaller amounts more frequently, rather than a lump sum once a year. Our platform will help capture this growing market for non-profits and solve the operational pains of managing micro-donations.

Who we are

Flourish Technologies would like to introduce a new mobile alternative donating platform for NPOs and their constituents. Users would own accounts that employ “round-up” technology to collect the difference between a debit or credit card purchase and the nearest dollar.

Benefits

Flourish is the best of both worlds. It allows donors to choose charities and projects that resonate with them, and it allows non-profits to transparently show the progress being made and keep track of their donors.
